CONTROL SYSTEM

One view of the whole power site.

PowerOps reads field equipment, shows current operating conditions, prepares control requests and keeps the full command history in one place.

01 · READ

Live equipment data

Voltage, frequency, load, fuel, alarms and operating state are collected from connected field equipment.

02 · APPROVE

Operator-controlled actions

Control requests stay blocked until an authorized operator reviews the readings and approves the action.

03 · VERIFY

Field confirmation

After dispatch, the equipment is read again and checked against the requested result.
